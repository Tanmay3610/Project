📜 The Official
Fiancé Constitution

A light-hearted guide for smooth communication, better bonding, food-related diplomacy, emotional safety, random teasing, and the gradual formation of a legendary team.

👨 Drafted by: Future Husband
👩 Pending Review by: Future Wife (Main Authority)

Preamble

We, the two future responsible-but-slightly-chaotic individuals, in order to build peace, comfort, fun, food, understanding, and occasional harmless drama, do hereby establish this Official Fiancé Constitution 😄

“Good bonding does not happen in one day, but little by little, it becomes something beautiful.”

Articles of Understanding

Every healthy future partnership deserves structure, silliness, and a proper legal framework for snacks, calls, and emotional compensation.

ARTICLE 1: Communication Rights

Section 1.1 — Daily Interaction
Both parties are encouraged to communicate regularly, depending on mood, energy, and life happening around them.

Section 1.2 — Good Morning / Good Night Clause
“Good Morning” and “Good Night” messages are appreciated and warmly welcomed, but shall not be treated as legally binding attendance records 😂

ARTICLE 2: Food & Snacks Policy

Section 2.1 — Favorite Dish Disclosure
Both parties must be transparent and honest regarding favorite foods, snacks, desserts, and comfort meals.

Section 2.2 — Controversial Food Clause
The status of Lauki ki Sabji and Matar Paneer shall remain under permanent review and may continue to be debated respectfully 😂

Section 2.3 — Future Taste Testing Rights
Both parties shall have equal rights to:

  • taste each other’s food,
  • give unnecessary food reviews,
  • and claim “mine is better” without proper evidence 😌

ARTICLE 3: Emotional Well-Being Clause

Section 3.1 — Concern Rights
If one party is unwell, stressed, upset, or having an “okayish” day, the other party shall retain full rights to say:

  • “Take care”
  • “Rest kar lo”
  • “Let me know if you need anything”

without sounding overdramatic 😄

Section 3.2 — Space & Comfort Balance
Both parties shall respect each other’s space, while also remembering that checking in sometimes is considered caring, not annoying 🙂

ARTICLE 4: Fun, Teasing & Random Activities

Section 4.1 — Rapid Fire Rights
Mini rapid fire rounds, “this or that”, and random fun chats are strongly encouraged.

Section 4.2 — Harmless Teasing Act
Light teasing shall be allowed, provided it remains:

  • cute,
  • respectful,
  • and non-traumatic 😌

Section 4.3 — April Fool Protection Law
Any April Fool prank attempted by either party must be harmless, funny, and emotionally safe 😂

Fake breakup announcements, panic creation, or heart attack-level jokes are strictly prohibited.

ARTICLE 5: Future Us Clause

Section 5.1 — Team Spirit
Both parties shall remember that they are not two people on opposite sides, but two people slowly learning how to become a good team ❤️

Section 5.2 — Baby Steps Policy
Since comfort and closeness take time, all progress shall be respected in the form of:

  • small conversations,
  • little efforts,
  • random smiles,
  • and growing ease with each other 😄

Section 5.3 — Important Reminder
It is officially recognized that progress counts even when it arrives softly.

ARTICLE 6: Special Regulations & Strictly Enforced Rules

Section 6.1 — “Ji” Usage Ban
Use of the word “Ji” in normal conversation is hereby declared strictly prohibited 🚫

Any unnecessary overuse of “Ji”, “Jii”, or “Jiiii” may be treated as:

  • excessive formality,
  • suspicious politeness,
  • or emotional distance 😌

Exception: Use of “Ji” may be allowed in extreme sarcasm, dramatic arguments, or intentional overacting 😂

ARTICLE 7: “Suiyan” Exposure Control Act

Section 7.1 — Safety Threshold
Use or prolonged exposure to “suiyan” (needles/injections) for more than 5 minutes is strictly prohibited 🚫😵

Any violation of this clause may result in:

  • panic,
  • irrational discomfort,
  • or immediate topic change demand 😄

Emergency Protocol
If “suiyan” exposure has crossed the officially permitted threshold of 5 minutes, the other party shall be legally bound to provide immediate emotional compensation.

  • one cute apology 😌
  • one distraction via food-related topic 🍲
  • one funny reel / meme 😂
  • one approved comforting message 😄
  • or one random picture under Fiancé Privileges 📸

ARTICLE 8: Photo & Appearance Rights

Section 8.1 — Picture Request Privilege
Occasional requests for selfies and random pictures shall be considered valid under Fiancé Privileges 😄

Section 8.2 — No Overthinking Rule
Statements such as:

  • “Achi nahi aa rahi”
  • “Main weird lag raha/rahi hoon”

shall be gently challenged, as both parties are expected to cooperate 😂

ARTICLE 9: PARANORMAL & HORROR SAFETY REGULATIONS

Section 9.1 — Horror Content Exposure Clause
If one party voluntarily introduces the other party to:

  • horror movies,
  • creepy books,
  • paranormal reels,
  • paranormal reels,

they shall also bear full responsibility for the emotional consequences
that follow and shall be legally bound to provide comfort in the form of: 👀

  • one reassuring message/call 😌
  • one funny distraction 😂
  • one approved “ye sab fake hai” explanation
  • or one non-horror topic immediately 🍲
